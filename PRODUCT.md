# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Individuals and couples seeking advanced medical information about fertility, IVF, and reproductive health. They are often stressed, anxious, and looking for credible, expert guidance.

## Product Purpose
To serve as an independent, premium platform ("إخصاب وإنجاب") specialized in spreading advanced medical awareness about fertility technologies, embryology, and reproductive health.

## Brand Personality
Professional, Empathetic, Scientific, Premium, Cinematic.

## Anti-references
- Cheap or generic local clinic templates
- Overly playful or bright SaaS designs
- Cluttered, text-heavy medical portals that look like 90s forums
- Designs that lack emotional warmth or look too strictly "corporate"

## Design Principles
1. **Expert Confidence:** The design should immediately convey world-class medical authority.
2. **Empathy through Design:** Use spacing, color, and typography to create a calm, reassuring environment for anxious users.
3. **Clarity in Complexity:** Medical information must be presented in a highly readable, digestible format without overwhelming the user.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- High contrast for maximum readability (WCAG AA).
- Clear, readable typography (especially for Arabic script).
- Support for reduced motion to avoid overwhelming users.
